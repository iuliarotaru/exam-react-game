About us

More than 300 games

Gaming Kitchen is a game publisher and game developer the platform has more than 300 games for kids and it is constantly growing. The website also has videos to watch like cartoons, gameplay videos, and game walkthroughs. The media catalog is growing daily.

As Gaming Kitchen has a long history, we have been recording the social phenomenon of browser games because games are an important artistic medium and can explain what people were like in a different time period.

What can you play here?

Gaming Kitchen is known for game genres like cute cartoon style and classic games. Another highly viewed category is the games for girls page with fashion, dress up, and sim games. More recently, the car games and 2 player games pages have grown in popularity. At Gaming Kitchen, we host thousands of older Flash games.

Some other exciting game engine platforms we support are WebGL and HTML5. Don't forget to register your GamingKitchen Account! It's the social network that supports the player community.
